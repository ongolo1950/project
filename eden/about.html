Our Story

EDEN Newspaper was established in 2004 as an English-language newspaper serving the Southwest Region of Cameroon, with Mile II, Limbe as its primary base. Our name "EDEN" reflects our commitment to providing a garden of truth and information to our readers.

Published bi-weekly on Mondays and Wednesdays, we have grown from a local publication to a respected regional newspaper, known for our unbiased reporting, in-depth analysis, and commitment to journalistic integrity. We cover everything from local community news to national and international events that affect our readers, serving as the "Best Cameroon News portal."

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver accurate, balanced, and comprehensive news coverage that informs, educates, and empowers our community. We strive to:

  • Provide factual and unbiased reporting on local, national, and international events
  • Give voice to the community and highlight important social issues
  • Promote transparency and accountability in governance
  • Support economic development and business growth in our region
  • Celebrate our cultural heritage and promote social cohesion
